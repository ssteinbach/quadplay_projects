quadplay✜ supports keyboard, touch controls, mouse, Xbox One, Xbox 360, PS4, 8BitDo, Switch, and Stadia controllers for input, and USB MIDI devices for input and output.

Press the "P" key or "☰", "Options", or "Start" controller button to pause and configure controllers and the system.
